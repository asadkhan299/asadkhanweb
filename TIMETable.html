Tips for Effective Time Management

  • Follow your timetable consistently but stay flexible for unexpected events.
  • Include short breaks to avoid burnout and stay fresh.
  • Prioritize important tasks first.
  • Adjust your schedule weekly based on your progress.
  • Use apps or planners to track tasks and study goals.
